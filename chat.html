How to Use Chat AI

Welcome to Chat AI! You can interact with the AI by typing your messages below. Here are some commands and inputs the AI recognizes:

  • Hello, How are you? - Standard greetings that the AI will respond to warmly.
  • /help - Get help about how to interact with the AI.
  • /schedule [seconds] [message] - Schedule a message to be sent after a specified number of seconds. Example: "/schedule 10 Hello there!" sends "Hello there!" after 10 seconds.
  • Type any other message to see how the AI will respond. It tries to understand and reply meaningfully!
